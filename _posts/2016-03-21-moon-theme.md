The Tour of Flanders 
(Dutch: Ronde van Vlaanderen),
 also known as De Ronde ("The Tour"), 
is an annual road cycling race held in 
Belgium every spring. 
The most important cycling 
race in Flanders this year 
starts in Antwerp.
